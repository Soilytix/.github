Soilytix is a Hamburg-based soil biology company. We combine **in-house DNA sequencing**, **soil chemistry**, and **machine learning** to turn the soil microbiome into decisions that improve yield, cut inputs, and rebuild soil health.

We built Soilytix to give soil biology a voice — not vague markers or noisy dashboards, but **transparent, research-grade data** you can act on in the field.

## What we do

| | |
|---|---|
| **Measure** | Standardized soil properties (texture, pH, SOM, structure) plus high-throughput DNA/RNA profiling, with rigorous lab QC |
| **Connect** | Link biology to outcomes — yield, quality, inputs, carbon — using geospatial sampling and field performance data |
| **Decide** | Field-specific recommendations on variety placement, nutrient strategy, timing, and soil-health progress |

From sample collection to agronomic insight, the workflow runs through **MySoilApp** (sampling in the field) and **MySoilDashboard** (exploration, benchmarking, and recommendations).

## Who we work with

- **Farmers & cooperatives** — placement, input efficiency, and soil-health tracking
- **Agri-input & seed companies** — product–soil fit, trials, and go-to-market targeting
- **Research institutions** — rigorous studies on microbiome, carbon, and management effects
- **Land investors & restoration** — biodiversity baselines, regeneration metrics, and reporting (CSRD, TNFD, EU soil monitoring)

## How we work

- **Own lab** — sequencing and soil analysis under one roof, built for production scale
- **Transparent analytics** — indicators, uncertainties, and trade-offs behind every recommendation
- **ROI-minded** — insights tied to practical levers and measurable outcomes (yield stability, input €/ha, SOM, biodiversity indices)

Better soils. Better decisions. Better outcomes.
